# Part one 

**The first three chapters provide a theoretical foundation and an overview of working with digital sustainability. Among other things, you will get answers to the following questions:**

1. What is sustainability and why is it important right now?
2. What concepts are essential when working with sustainability in digital practices?
3. Why is systems thinking a common methodology for both digital development and sustainability development?
4. Where do we stand in relation to the impact of digitalization on the environment and society?
